Mouse_response items

Mouse_response items work analogously to the keyboard_response items, with the difference that mouse clicks are recorded. There are 5 types of mouse clicks, which can be used as Correct response or Allowed responses.

  • left_button or 1

  • middle_button or 2

  • right_button or 3

  • scroll_up or 4

  • scroll_down or 5

For more information, please refer to the help page of the keyboard_response item.
